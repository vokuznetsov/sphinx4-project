1. How to convert binary files to text files from acoustic model?
Download sphinx_train and launch the following command: printp.exe -*param* *file*.

param			file
-gaufn			means
-gaufn			variances
-mixwfn			mixture_weights
-tmatfn 		transition_matrices

https://sourceforge.net/p/cmusphinx/discussion/help/thread/1f9fdb0f/
https://sourceforge.net/p/cmusphinx/discussion/sphinx4/thread/73bb9606/?limit=25#d201/5f3e/2f35

Ex. printp.exe -gaufn variances > variances.txt
